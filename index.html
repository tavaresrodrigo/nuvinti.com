About Nuvinti

Nuvinti is revolutionizing the cloud computing landscape in Latin America by combining cutting-edge technology with environmental responsibility. Founded with the vision of creating a truly sustainable digital infrastructure, we are pioneering the development of data centers powered entirely by renewable energy.

Our approach goes beyond traditional cloud services - we're building an ecosystem that supports local economies, promotes digital sovereignty, and protects the environment for future generations. Through our innovative solar-powered data centers, we're proving that high-performance computing and environmental stewardship can work hand in hand.

Our Mission

Our mission is to democratize secure and affordable cloud computing in Latin America, pioneering truly sustainable data centers built with 100% solar energy and ecological materials.

We are committed to fostering local economic growth and digital independence across the region, ensuring that cutting-edge technology is accessible to all while protecting our planet's future.
